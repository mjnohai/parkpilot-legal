# ParkPilot Support

**support@getparkpilot.com**

We typically respond within 1–2 business days.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**How do recommendations work?**  
ParkPilot's engine scores every open attraction using live wait times, your 
walking distance, party composition, heat conditions, and your must-do list. 
The top pick at any moment is the one most likely to give you the best 
experience given current conditions.

**How long does the free trial last?**  
The free trial begins on your first recommendation request and lasts 2 hours. 
After that, a ParkPilot Pro subscription is required to continue.

**I subscribed but the app still shows the paywall.**  
Tap "Already a subscriber? Restore" on the paywall screen. If the issue 
persists, force-quit the app and reopen it.

**I got a new phone. How do I restore my Pro subscription?**  
Open ParkPilot, go through onboarding for your park, and when the paywall 
appears tap "Already a subscriber? Restore". Your subscription will be 
detected automatically.

**The map won't open.**  
Make sure Apple Maps or Google Maps is installed. Tap "Open in Maps" and 
select your preferred app if prompted.

**How do I cancel my subscription?**  
Subscriptions are managed by Apple. Go to **Settings → Apple ID → 
Subscriptions** on your iPhone to cancel or modify your ParkPilot Pro 
subscription.

**My question isn't answered here.**  
Email us at [support@getparkpilot.com](mailto:support@getparkpilot.com) and 
we'll get back to you.
