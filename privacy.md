# Privacy Policy

**ParkPilot**  
Last updated: June 2026

---

## Who We Are

ParkPilot is a theme-park day companion app that uses an AI recommendation engine to suggest your next ride or
attraction based on real-time wait times, your location, party type, and preferences.

ParkPilot is an independent app and is not affiliated with The Walt Disney Company or any park operator.

---

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide

- **Park and visit preferences** — the park you are visiting, party type, mobility needs, pace preference, and
  attractions on your must-do list.
- **Planned departure time** — used to pace recommendations throughout your park day.

### Information Collected Automatically

- **Approximate location** — your device location is sent with recommendation requests so we can score attractions by
  walking distance. We do not store your location history. Location is used only to generate current recommendations and
  is then discarded.
- **Device identifier** — a random UUID generated on your device and stored in your iOS Keychain. This identifier helps
  restore ParkPilot Pro access on a new device. It is not linked to your Apple ID, name, or email address.
- The app also stores small operational counters on your device (such as how many times you have used the app and
  whether you have been prompted to leave a review). These are stored locally in iOS Keychain, are never transmitted to
  our servers, and contain no personally identifiable information.

### Information Received from Apple

- **App Store purchase information** — when you subscribe to ParkPilot Pro, StoreKit purchase data is sent to our
  servers so we can verify your subscription with Apple.
- **Subscription records** — we store subscription status, expiration information, and a stable Apple transaction
  identifier so we can recognize valid subscription access across devices.

---

## How We Use Your Information

| Data                    | Purpose                                        |
|-------------------------|------------------------------------------------|
| Location                | Score ride recommendations by walking distance |
| Visit preferences       | Personalize recommendations for your group     |
| Device identifier       | Help restore Pro status on a new device        |
| App Store purchase data | Verify and unlock subscriptions                |

We use your information only to operate, secure, and improve ParkPilot.

We do not:

- Sell your personal information
- Rent or trade your personal information
- Use your information for advertising
- Track you across other companies' apps or websites

---

## Information Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information.

Your data may be shared only with:

- **Our backend infrastructure** — to generate recommendations and verify purchases
- **Apple** — to validate App Store subscriptions as required by Apple's in-app purchase system
- **Service providers acting on our behalf** — only to the extent necessary to securely host and operate ParkPilot

All data is transmitted over HTTPS.

We do not use third-party advertising SDKs or social media integrations.

---

## Data Retention

- **Session data** (preferences, must-do attractions, completed attractions, and related recommendation data) expires
  automatically after 24 hours.
- **Subscription records** are retained while needed to validate or restore an active subscription, or until deletion is
  requested where applicable.
- **Location coordinates** are not stored after recommendation processing is complete.

---

## Your Rights

You may request deletion of data associated with your device identifier by contacting us at the email address below.

Because we do not collect your name or email address within the app, we may ask you to provide your device identifier so
we can locate your records. The device identifier can be found in the Settings section of the app.

Please note that deleting subscription-related records may affect our ability to help restore ParkPilot Pro access on a
future device.

---

## Children's Privacy

ParkPilot is intended for users aged 13 and older.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided
information through the app, please contact us and we will promptly delete it.

---

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time as the app evolves.

When changes are made, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of ParkPilot after
updated policies are posted constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
