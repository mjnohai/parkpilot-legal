# Privacy Policy

**ParkPilot**
Last updated: June 2026

---

## Who We Are

ParkPilot is a theme-park day companion app that uses an AI recommendation
engine to suggest your next ride or attraction based on real-time wait times,
your location, party type, and preferences. We are an independent developer
and not affiliated with The Walt Disney Company or any park operator.

---

## Information We Collect

### Information you provide

- **Park and visit preferences** — the park you're visiting, party type,
  mobility needs, pace preference, and the attractions on your must-do list.
  You enter this during onboarding.
- **Planned departure time** — so we can pace recommendations around your day.

### Information collected automatically

- **Approximate location** — your GPS coordinates are sent with each
  recommendation request to score attractions by walking distance. We do not
  store your location history; coordinates are used only to compute the
  current recommendation and discarded.
- **Device identifier** — a random UUID generated on your device and stored
  in your iOS Keychain. This is used to restore your Pro subscription when
  you get a new phone. It is not linked to your Apple ID, name, or email
  address.

### Information from Apple

- **App Store receipt** — when you subscribe to ParkPilot Pro, a StoreKit 2
  signed receipt is sent to our server to verify your purchase with Apple.
  We store the subscription expiry date and a stable Apple transaction
  identifier to recognise your subscription across devices.

---

## How We Use Your Information

| Data              | Purpose                                        |
|-------------------|------------------------------------------------|
| Location          | Score ride recommendations by walking distance |
| Visit preferences | Personalise recommendations to your group      |
| Device identifier | Restore Pro status on a new device             |
| App Store receipt | Verify and unlock your Pro subscription        |

We do not use your information for advertising, profiling, or any purpose
other than operating the app.

---

## Information Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information.

Your data is shared only with:

- **Our backend servers** — to generate recommendations and verify purchases.
  Data is transmitted over HTTPS.
- **Apple** — to verify your App Store purchase, as required by Apple's
  in-app purchase system.

We do not use third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or social media
integrations.

---

## Data Retention

- **Session data** (preferences, must-dos, completed attractions) expires
  automatically after 24 hours.
- **Subscription records** are retained until your subscription expires or
  you request deletion.
- **Location coordinates** are not stored — they are used only at the moment
  a recommendation is generated.

---

## Your Rights

You may request deletion of any data associated with your device identifier
by contacting us at the address below. Because we do not collect your name
or email, we will ask you to provide your device identifier (found in
Settings within the app) to locate your records.

---

## Children's Privacy

ParkPilot is intended for users aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly
collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child
under 13 has provided information through the app, please contact us and we
will delete it promptly.

---

changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
